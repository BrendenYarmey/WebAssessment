We will be discussing some general informtion about snakes below.


There are a total of 3789 snake species and they all live to be between 5 to 30 years old, depending on the species and enviornment.

Another interesting fact is that the largest known snake gathering in the world is actually right here in Manitoba and invloves thousands of garter snakes in the spring.

Two more unique features snakes have include having no eyelids and smelling with their tongues.
